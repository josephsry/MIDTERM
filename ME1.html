Inception

Inception is a 2010 science fiction action film directed by Christopher Nolan. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as a professional thief who steals information by infiltrating the subconscious of his targets. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for a seemingly impossible task: planting an idea in a target's subconscious. The film explores the nature of dreams and reality, as well as the concept of shared dreaming. Source: Wikipedia.

The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. The film stars Christian Bale as Batman and Heath Ledger as the Joker. The film follows Batman as he tries to stop the Joker, who is wreaking havoc on Gotham City. The film explores themes of chaos and order, as well as the nature of heroism. Source: Wikipedia.

Pulp Fiction

Pulp Fiction is a 1994 crime film directed by Quentin Tarantino. The film is known for its nonlinear narrative structure and its eclectic mix of characters and dialogue. The film stars John Travolta and Samuel L. Jackson as hitmen who carry out various jobs for their boss, played by Ving Rhames. The film explores themes of violence, redemption, and pop culture. Source: Wikipedia.

Forrest Gump

Forrest Gump is a 1994 drama film directed by Robert Zemeckis. The film stars Tom Hanks as the titular character, a man with a low IQ who achieves great success in his life despite his challenges. The film follows Forrest as he becomes a football star, joins the army, and meets various historical figures. The film explores themes of love, loss, and the American experience. Source: Wikipedia.

The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 drama film directed by Frank Darabont. The film stars Tim Robbins as a banker who is sentenced to life in prison for the murder of his wife and her lover, and Morgan Freeman as a fellow prisoner. The film follows their friendship and the ways in which they cope with their incarceration. The film explores themes of hope, redemption, and the human spirit. Source: Wikipedia.
